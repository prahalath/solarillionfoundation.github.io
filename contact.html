We are currently accepting applications for Under Graduate Research Assistants and Full Time Research Assistants. Undergraduate students (in any year) in circuit branches (EEE, ECE, E&I, CSE, ICE and BME), CSE/IT can apply. Students can also apply for the position of Full-Time Research Assistants. Ideally Full Time Research Assistants will join in their 7’th/8’th Semester of studies and continue after graduation. Young Professionals with 1-2 years work experience that want to join our research program before applying for graduate studies are also welcome to apply.

You can apply to the program here.

We follow a rolling admissions deadline. You are encouraged to apply early, as admissions will close once positions are filled.

If you have any questions regarding the admission process or the program, or if you believe your question has still not been answered drop us an email at sfadmissions2016 (at) gmail {dot} com.
